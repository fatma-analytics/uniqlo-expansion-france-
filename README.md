## 🚀 Project Overview

Uniqlo, a leader in Japanese fast-fashion, has a strong but concentrated presence in France. This project evaluates its current market position and proposes a **Differentiated Expansion Strategy** based on regional productivity metrics and competitive gaps.

### 🎯 Strategic Objectives
- **Market Assessment**: Analyzing Uniqlo's internal strengths (98% profit margin) and external threats (Zara, H&M).
- **Expansion Modeling**: Comparing uniform vs. differentiated store opening scenarios.
- **Productivity Analysis**: Using transaction-per-employee metrics to identify high-potential regions.
- **Market Share Optimization**: Targeting a 30% national market share through strategic regional entry.

---

## 📊 Key Insights & Methodology

### 1. Internal & External Analysis
The project utilizes the **SWOT Framework** and **Porter's Five Forces** to establish a baseline:
- **Strengths**: Exceptional operational efficiency and brand loyalty.
- **Competitive Rivalry**: High pressure from Inditex (Zara) and H&M, requiring a focus on quality and innovation (LifeWear).
- **Substitutes**: Growing demand for sustainable and second-hand fashion alternatives.

### 2. Data-Driven Expansion Scenarios
Two primary scenarios were modeled to forecast impact:
- **Uniform Strategy**: Opening 2 stores per region (conservative growth).
- **Differentiated Strategy (Recommended)**: Allocating resources based on regional productivity tiers (e.g., prioritizing Grand Est and Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes).

### 3. Power BI Visualizations
The analysis is supported by interactive dashboards (see `final- 0211.pbix`) focusing on:
- **Sales Evolution (2021-2024)**: Tracking turnover growth across 9 key regions.
- **Market Share Comparison**: Visualizing Uniqlo's position against Zara, H&M, and Mango.
- **Regional Productivity**: Mapping the "av. use rate of salesmen" to identify under-served markets.

## 🏆 Recommendations
- **Prioritize High-Productivity Regions**: Focus expansion on Group 1 regions (Grand Est, PACA) where transaction rates are highest.
- **Enter Untapped Markets**: Open stores in regions like Centre-Val de Loire where competitors are absent to capture 100% local market share.
- **Monitor KPIs**: Implement quarterly assessments of sales-per-employee and average transaction value to ensure sustainable growth.
